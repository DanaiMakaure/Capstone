    Why Students Struggle?

    Academic failure and dropout aren‚Äôt always caused by a lack of intelligence. Often, students silently struggle with behavioral challenges, low engagement, and a lack of timely support. Our project, Learning Under the Lens, aims to identify these hidden risk factors early through data-driven insights.

    • Low Engagement: Minimal participation in class activities, group work, or platforms ‚Äî often the first sign of disengagement.
    • Poor Attendance: Skipping lectures or online sessions, leading to missed learning opportunities and gaps in understanding.
    • Inconsistent Study Habits: Irregular or insufficient study hours that weaken long-term retention and exam readiness.
    • Stress and Burnout: Mental fatigue or personal challenges that affect motivation, focus, and overall performance.
    • Lack of Feedback: Students unaware of their learning gaps or progress are less likely to improve without guidance.
    • Delayed Intervention: Teachers and institutions failing to detect risks early enough to act meaningfully.
